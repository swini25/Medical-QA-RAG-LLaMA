Myth: Vaccines cause autism.
Fact: Multiple studies have shown that vaccines do not cause autism. The original study that claimed this has been debunked and retracted.

Myth: You can catch a cold from being cold.
Fact: Colds are caused by viruses, not temperature. However, cold weather may weaken the immune response, increasing susceptibility.

Myth: Sugar causes hyperactivity in children.
Fact: There is no scientific evidence that sugar causes hyperactivity. This belief is largely a myth perpetuated by anecdotal reports.
